## Evidence Basis Tiers

- **Confirmed** — Directly measured or observed during the audit crawl. Backed by specific evidence (page weight, DOM counts, load times, etc.).
- **Reviewer-Identified** — Flagged as a coverage gap by the reviewer, then investigated and verified by the auditor. Real issues, but without direct crawl measurements.
- **Needs Verification** — Inferred without a direct measurement or observed selector. Likely real, but verify against the live page/source before acting.
